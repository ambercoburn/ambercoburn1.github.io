Bluehost

Bluehost is described as having the best uptime at 99.99%, fast load time at 0.41ms, and a cheap intro cost at $2.75 per month. Additionally, it is easy to use and signup for. Fast support and a 30-day money back gurantee make it a top web host. However, one down fall is there is no free site mirgration. One cannot transfer their existing website to their hosting environment.

HostGator

Hostgator is popular because it has the most unlimited hosting. People are able to have unlimited email accounts and free site migration. Additionally, the uptime is 99.98% and load time is 0.43ms. The downfall is a higher renewal cost at $2.99 per month and after the first 6 months it increases to $9.95 per month minimum.

Hostinger

Hostinger is considered the cheapest web hosting at $0.80 per month. People are allowed one free domain name for a year. The load time is 0.37ms and uptime is slower than the two listed above at 99.91%. There is a 30 day money back gurantee, as the price can increase with the more you add on.

Top Host

If I had to pick a top host I would go with blue host because I think it is the most user friendly for building new websites. It has a website builder for beginners, a good price for what is included, and if I do not like it I can get my money back before 30 days.
